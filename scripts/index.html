Nayeon first performed a cover of Ikimonogakari's "Kimagure Romantic" at BEAT AX Vol.5 on February 2, 2025. The performance quickly went viral, doubling the original song's daily streams in Japan from 22,000 to 44,000. It also entered Spotify Japan and other major Japanese charts for the first time. Following the surge in popularity, the original artists asked Nayeon to release her own version, which was officially released on May 30.

IM NAYEON

Nayeon is a South Korean singer known as the lead vocalist, lead dancer, center, and face of the girl group TWICE. She was the first member of TWICE to launch a solo career, releasing two solo albums: IM NAYEON on June 24, 2022, and NA on June 14, 2024.
